about this website
This site was designed and built by @ahoefinger, and the source code can be found on github. When I first discovered Buzzfeed's quizzes, I became really interested in figuring out how they work and building one of my own. I thought a quiz about Morrissey would be fun, mainly because I learned a lot of trivia about his life and music from reading his autobiography, and also because I saw that there is an abundance of hunky photos of Morrissey on the internet.
